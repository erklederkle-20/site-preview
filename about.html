About Me

Hello! My name is Erik, and I’m the creator of SpideyPhysics. I’m passionate about making physics and mathematics easy, fun, and engaging for students of all levels.

Who I Am

I’m currently working on a Master’s in Nanoscience and Nanotechnology, I completed an undergraduate degree in traditional physics, and I’ve spent years teaching and tutoring physics, mathematics, and related subjects. I like to think that anyone can learn physics with enough time and practice!

My Mission

SpideyPhysics exists to help students untangle complex concepts, build confidence, and truly enjoy the process of learning. Whether you’re studying for school, university, or personal interest — there’s something here for you.
