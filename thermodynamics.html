Thermodynamics

Introduction

Thermodynamics is the study of heat, energy, and work, focusing on the laws governing energy transfer and the properties of systems in equilibrium.

Recommended Textbooks

  • Thermodynamics: An Engineering Approach – Yunus A. Çengel & Michael A. Boles
  • Fundamentals of Statistical and Thermal Physics – Frederick Reif
  • Introduction to Modern Thermodynamics – Dilip Kondepudi
