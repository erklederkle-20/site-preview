Mechanics

Introduction

Mechanics is the branch of physics that deals with the motion of objects and the forces that affect them. It forms the foundation for understanding how things move in everyday life and in the universe.

Recommended Textbooks

  • Classical Mechanics – Herbert Goldstein
  • Introduction to Mechanics – Kleppner and Kolenkow
  • Fundamentals of Physics – Halliday, Resnick, and Walker
