Electromagnetism

Introduction

Electromagnetism studies electric and magnetic fields and how they interact with charged particles. It is fundamental to understanding electricity, magnetism, and light.

Recommended Textbooks

  • Introduction to Electrodynamics – David J. Griffiths
  • Electricity and Magnetism – Edward M. Purcell
  • Classical Electrodynamics – John D. Jackson
